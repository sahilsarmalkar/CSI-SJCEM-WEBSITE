About Us

We are the C.S.I. Student Branch at St. John College of Engineering and Management.CSI-SJCEM is a dedicated college organization that initiates workshops, events, and seminars to explore a vast range of information beyond the standard university curriculum. To enhance both technical and non-technical education, we organize numerous seminars and workshops led by professionals who share their expertise with students. We also encourage our members to plan and host their own events, fostering skills in management, self-confidence, and the exchange of ideas and information.
